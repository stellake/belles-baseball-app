Why join us?

Community
We aren’t just a team, we’re a family, and we support each other with everything. Good day? bad day? Need advice? You can bet that The Belles are there to support you.
Learning about the game
We LOVE baseball, and we love learning from each other. Collectively we have a wealth of knowledge about baseball, and we spend time together on and off (via zoom) the field working on our game, and bonding as a team.
Exercise
Get stronger with us. All you need to do is strive to be 1% better, and you will see results. We are proud to have a throwing programme that is available to our players, and have strength and conditioning sessions planned throughout the year.

New starter checklist

  • Register your interest using our online form
  • Make sure you are in our private Facebook group
  • Join our Whatsapp chat
  • Accept your Teamer invite
